Dynamic Lane Priority

Detects lane density and grants green time to the busiest lane; no confusing UI, just smarter cycles.

Emergency Vehicle Detection

Recognizes uploaded vehicle profiles and clears a path instantly for ambulances, fire trucks and police.

Green Corridor

Manual override for continuous green lanes; simple toggle for control room operators.

Obstacle Detection

Real-time detection of pedestrians/animals entering other lanes; reduces collision risk by pausing flow.

Emergency Handling

Automatically detect and prioritize emergency situations for rapid response and safety.

Map View

Real-time interactive maps showing traffic flow and incidents for better monitoring.

Manual Control

Reports

Generate detailed logs and traffic reports to analyze patterns and improve management.


How it works?

1

Monitor

Cameras / sensors collect per-lane vehicle counts and detect movement patterns in real-time.

2

Detect

AI model recognizes emergency vehicles, pedestrians, animals, and other anomalies on the road.

3

Decide

System determines lane priority and decides whether to maintain cycle, switch, or override for emergencies.

4

Act & Log

Signals update instantly; events are logged for audit and later analysis (helps traffic police review incidents).


Why choose Smart Traffic?

Saves Time

Less idle time at signals — more consistent throughput for busy intersections.

Prioritizes Emergencies

Faster response for ambulances and fire units — critical minutes saved.

Safer Roads

Stops traffic when obstructions appear, reducing collision risk and injuries.

Data Driven

Logs and reports give police insights to improve signal timing and city planning.
